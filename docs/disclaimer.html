Data Source

All property records displayed in this tool are sourced from publicly accessible county GIS portals maintained by Bedford County, Lynchburg City, Campbell County, Amherst County, and Appomattox County, Virginia. These records are public information available to any member of the public under the Virginia Freedom of Information Act (FOIA).

Purpose

This tool is used solely for non-commercial religious outreach by One In Christ Church (OIC), a local church in Forest, Virginia. It is intended to help church members welcome new neighbors in the community.

No data is sold, shared with third parties, or used for any commercial purpose.

Ethnicity Estimates

Ethnicity labels shown on this site are statistical estimates derived from US Census Bureau surname frequency data using the Bayesian Improved Surname Geocoding (BISG) model. These are population-level probabilities, not verified individual facts.

Estimates may be inaccurate for any given individual. They are used solely to help prioritize outreach in alignment with the church's multi-ethnic mission and Chinese-language programs.

Accuracy

Household size, move origin, and other estimates are approximations based on available public data. No guarantee is made regarding their accuracy.

Privacy

This site is not indexed by search engines. Notes, visit records, and follow-up flags are stored only in your device's local browser storage and are never transmitted to any server.
